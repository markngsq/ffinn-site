About the Workshops

  • CDJ-oriented DJ workshops
  • Beginner and Intermediate skill levels
  • 1-on-1 and small groups
  • In-person at Pirate Studios – Bristol, UK

My workshops are informed by my acquired skills and experience as a neurodiverse, queer, femme-presenting selector and technician in the industry for over a decade. I will be working with your personal goals and access needs, so you can expect a safe, informal, organic and playful space that's free of judgement, where mistakes are seen as the best learning points.

If you'd like to kick-start or progress your DJ journey, convert your vinyl skills over to CDJs, and/or venture into crafting multi-genre sets seamlessly — I'd love to meet you!

Topics I Cover

but not limited to:

  • Anatomy of a song
  • Counting bars
  • Rekordbox 101 Pt.1 — Essentials to get your USBs ready for CDJs
  • Rekordbox 101 Pt.2 — Advanced features to streamline your use of CDJs
  • Equipment 101 Pt.1 — Familiarisation of the standard CDJ setup
  • Equipment 101 Pt.2 — Advanced use to introduce more flair to your sets
  • Lining up tracks / track cueing
  • Beat matching
  • Mixing with EQs
  • Finding the right cue point(s)
  • Mixing nuances & flairs
  • Recovering from mistakes
  • Curating a mixtape and/or multi-genre journey
  • DJ etiquette
  • Insights from a neurodiverse, queer, femme-presenting DJ, artist, technician, former record label owner and promoter

Lessons are held in-person at Pirate Studios in Bristol, UK. Prices include studio hire.
