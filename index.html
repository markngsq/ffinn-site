About the Workshops

  • CDJ-oriented DJ workshops
  • Beginner and Intermediate skill levels
  • 1-on-1 and small groups
  • In-person at Pirate Studios – Bristol, UK

My workshops are informed by my acquired skills and experience as a neurodiverse, queer, femme-presenting selector and technician in the industry for over a decade. I will be working with your personal goals and access needs, so you can expect a safe, informal, organic and playful space that's free of judgement, where mistakes are seen as the best learning points.

If you'd like to kick-start or progress your DJ journey, convert your vinyl skills over to CDJs, and/or venture into crafting multi-genre sets seamlessly — I'd love to meet you!

About FFINN

You can call me Fiona or Fi (they/she)

  • Professional DJ for 10+ years
  • DJ tutor for 5+ years
  • Enhanced DBS checked
  • Affiliations: Varispeed, Mothers Against Noise, SLUG WIFE
  • Notable past appearances: Shambala Festival, Bang Face Weekender, Balter Festival, Hit & Run UK

My unique skill lies in multi-genre musical journeys that balance the dark atmospheric and silly energy, creating relatable and memorable moments for any environment. Since finding my voice, I've been invited to share stages with acts such as Ben Pest, Itoa, Monuman, The DJ Producer and Samurai Breaks.

I enjoy an eclectic list of genres, including but not limited to: Dubstep/140, DnB/Halftime, Jungle, Juke/Footwork, Garage, Electro, Breaks, House/Techno, Trance, IDM/Electronica, Hip Hop, Post-Rock, Indie, Metal and all kinds of Pop music.

Check out my mixes and see if our vibes match! 🙂
